## 🔭 What I'm working on

**Engineer & project lead** at [OPHTAO](https://ophtao.fr), an ophthalmology practice in Houilles (78).

- 🌐 Shipped the practice's public website and a private module for staff and administrative management
- 🔌 Built tooling that extends **StudioVision**, the practice's medical records software
- 🌉 Deployed **AlmaPro** and automated a bridge between legacy patient records and France's *Dossier Médical Partagé* — the SEGUR-certified path StudioVision never had
- 🔬 R&D: literature review and preliminary experiments on AI for retinal imaging
- 🛠️ And the unglamorous half of the job: configuring ophthalmic measurement devices, and being the person people call when nothing works

Most of the full-stack work is done pairing with Claude.

## 🧠 Where I've been

**LLM Engineer** · Agence du Numérique en Santé × Inria × Inserm (HeKA) · *2025*
Combining LLMs with knowledge graphs to translate SNOMED CT into French. Multi-RAG in-context learning, structural retrieval over the concept graph, cosine similarity across concept embeddings, and a lot of hands-on time with Aya 13B.

**Prompt Engineer** · Lariboisière Hospital, Anesthesiology · *2024*
Extracting structured biology data from anesthesia consultation notes: patient record anonymisation, clinical text segmentation, terms and values out into DataFrames.

**Data Scientist** · Institut de Biologie Paris-Seine, Sorbonne Université · *2022*
Automating action-matrix definition for genomic data analysis, plus clustering algorithms and a first taste of how research actually gets done.
